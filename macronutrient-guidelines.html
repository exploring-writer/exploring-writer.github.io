Macronutrient Guidelines

1. Protein (단백질)

  • Energy: 4 kcal/g
  • Daily target: 70g (20% of 1400 kcal)
  • Per meal limit: 25-30g (essential amino acids >2.2g)

Optimal Intake Guidelines

  • By body weight:
    • Minimum: 0.8g per kg (below this leads to muscle loss)
    • Optimal metabolism: 1-1.2g per kg
    • During diet: 1.2-1.6g per kg
    • Muscle synthesis: 1-1.5g per kg
    • Safe upper limit: 2.0g per kg (no kidney damage observed)
  • By calorie percentage:
    • Optimal longevity: 15-20%
    • Korean guidelines: 7-20%
    • US guidelines: 10-35%
    • Consensus range: 15-20%

Risks of Extreme Diets

  • Too low: Muscle loss
  • Too high: Kidney strain, excess protein converts to fat

2. Carbohydrates (탄수화물)

  • Energy: 4 kcal/g
  • Daily target: 200g (55% of 1400 kcal)
  • Sugar limit: <35g per day

Recommended Ranges

  • Korean hospital guidelines: 55-70%
  • US hospital guidelines: 45-65%
  • Longevity studies suggest: ~50%
  • Chosen target: 55% (lower range due to higher protein needs)

Risks of Extreme Diets

  • Too low: Early onset dementia risk (brain requires carbs)
  • Too high: Diabetes risk, accelerated aging

Sugar Guidelines

  • Limit: <35g per day (10% of daily calories)
  • Some sources recommend <25g
  • Focus on natural sugars (e.g., from fruits) over added sugars
  • Minimize added sugars (syrups, processed sugars)

3. Fats (지방)

  • Energy: 9 kcal/g
  • Daily target: 35g (25% of 1400 kcal)
  • Saturated fat limit: <11g per day

Fat Types and Guidelines

  • Total fat: 20-30% of calories
    • Calculation: (1400 - (70×4) - (200×4)) = 320 kcal
    • Result: 35g (23% of total calories)
  • Saturated fats (solid at room temperature):
    • Limit to <7% of calories (<11g)
    • Can contribute to blood thickness and vessel blockage
  • Unsaturated fats (liquid at room temperature):
    • Essential nutrients
    • Aids blood circulation
    • Should make up majority of fat intake
  • Trans fats:
    • Limit to <2g (ideally avoid completely)
    • Common in processed foods and restaurant meals
    • Difficult for body to process and eliminate

Risks of Extreme Diets

  • Too low: Brain function decline, hair loss (brain is ~60% fat)
  • Too high: Cardiovascular diseases, atherosclerosis, stroke risk

Summary (1400 kcal Diet)

  • Carbs: 200g (55%, 800 kcal)
  • Protein: 70g (20%, 280 kcal)
  • Fat: 35g (25%, 320 kcal)

Note: These are guidelines, not strict rules. Use them to avoid extreme under or over consumption.
