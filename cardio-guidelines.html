HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

Structure

  • High-intensity intervals:
    • Duration: 30-60 seconds
    • Target heart rate: >147 (80% of max)
    • Note: No need to reach max heart rate within 1 minute
  • Recovery intervals:
    • Duration: 1-2 minutes
    • Target heart rate: 100-120 bpm
    • Goal: Return to 120 bpm or lower
  • Complete 7-10 sets

Anaerobic Training

Guidelines

  • Duration: 30-60 minutes
    • Avoid longer sessions (increases stress hormones)
  • Principles:
    • Fast and heavy
    • Avoid single-joint and machine exercises
    • RPE 8+ weight for 8-12 reps
  • Tabata Protocol:
    • 20 seconds maximum effort
    • 10 seconds rest
    • 8 sets total

Important Tips

  • Water and oxygen are ingredient of fat burning, so drink enough water and do aerobic exercise.
  • Afterburn effect can last up to 38 hours
  • Core exercises are best done on an empty stomach
  • Cardio requirements:
    • Minimum duration: 10 minutes
    • Effective results from 15 minutes
    • Should feel out of breath (no benefit if breathing is too comfortable)
  • High-intensity pattern:
    • Reach target heart rate
    • Rest for 1 minute
    • Repeat

Exercise Intensity Guide

  • Moderate Intensity:
    • Alternative range: 60-70% (110-129 bpm)
    • Can maintain conversation but with some effort
  • High Intensity:
    • Talking becomes difficult
    • Breathing is heavy but controlled
