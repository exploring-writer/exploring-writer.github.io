Common Infections Guidelines

COVID-19

  • Transmission: Through respiratory droplets
  • Droplet Behavior:
    • Though lightweight, droplets typically fall to the ground after traveling about 1.8 meters (6 feet)
    • This is why maintaining this distance for social distancing is recommended
  • Prevention:
    • Vaccination
    • Wearing masks
    • Social distancing
    • Stay home when sick to prevent spreading infections

Common Cold

  • Primary Transmission:
    • Through touching a surface contaminated withvirus-containing nasal discharge
    • Commonly transmitted as (infected person) hand -> object -> (uninfected person) hand -> face
  • Prevention:
    • Wash hands frequently, after using bathroom or returning from outside
    • Avoid eating with unwashed hands
