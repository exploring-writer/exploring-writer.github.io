Carbohydrate Sources Guidelines

Quality Criteria for Carbohydrate Sources

Two Main Factors:

  1. Absorption Speed
    • Slower absorption → Less insulin spike → Slower aging
    • Slower absorption → Better satiety → Less overeating
  2. Micronutrient Content
    • Vitamins and minerals are essential for health
    • Refined grains have significantly reduced micronutrient content

Good Sources: Whole Grains (통곡물)

  • Characteristics:
    • Slow absorption rate
    • Rich in micronutrients
    • Should form the majority of carbohydrate intake
  • Examples:
    • Brown rice (현미)
    • Barley (보리)
    • Wild rice
    • Quinoa (퀴노아)
    • Oats (오트)
    • Farro (파로)
    • Whole wheat (통밀)

Poor Sources

1. Refined Grains (정제곡물)

  • Characteristics:
    • Rapid absorption
    • Low in micronutrients (most removed during processing)
    • Some products are fortified with vitamins due to poor nutritional value
  • Examples:
    • White rice (흰쌀)
    • White bread (흰빵)
    • White noodles (흰국수)

2. Added Sugars (첨가당)

  • Basic added sugars:
    • Sugar (설탕)
    • Honey (꿀)
    • Rice syrup (조청)
    • Corn syrup (물엿)
  • Worst option - High Fructose Corn Syrup:
    • Names: 액상과당, 결정과당, 포도당 시럽, 고과당 옥수수 시럽, 콘시럽
    • Very rapid absorption → Direct path to diabetes
    • Common in processed foods and some coffee syrups
  • Common sources:
    • Most snacks
    • Candies
    • Ice cream
    • Many restaurant foods

Moderate Sources: Fruits

  • Pros:
    • Rich in micronutrients
    • High in fiber (slows sugar absorption)
    • Better alternative to snacks when craving sweets
  • Cons:
    • Contains simple sugars (fructose)
  • Recommendation:
    • Limit to 100-200g per day

Sugar Alternatives

  • Characteristics:
    • Not absorbed by body
    • No caloric accumulation
    • May cause diarrhea if consumed in excess
    • Some debate about appetite effects
    • Better than sugar, but moderation still important
  • Good-tasting options:
    • Allulose (알룰로스)
    • Monk fruit (나한과)
    • Erythritol (에리스리톨)
  • Less palatable:
    • Stevia (스테비아)

Practical Tips

Efficient Meal Prep Method:

  1. Prepare 2 weeks worth of mixed grains and beans
  2. Portion into single-serving containers
  3. Freeze portions
  4. Night before: Move portion to refrigerator to thaw
  5. Heat in microwave for 1:30 when ready to eat

Benefits: Time-saving and portion control
