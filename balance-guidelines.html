Ankle Exercises

Foundation of Balance

  • Basic Exercises:
    • Ankle circles (clockwise and counterclockwise)
    • Heel raises and toe lifts
    • Single-leg balance
  • Advanced Exercises:
    • Balance board training
    • Walking on toes and heels
    • Single-leg exercises with movement

Core Training

Center of Balance

  • Basic Exercises:
    • Planks (front and side)
    • Bird dog pose
    • Dead bug exercise
  • Benefits:
    • Improves posture
    • Enhances stability
    • Reduces risk of back pain

Specialized Activities

Comprehensive Balance Training

  • Pilates:
    • Focuses on core strength
    • Improves body awareness
    • Enhances flexibility and control
  • Yoga:
    • Balance-focused poses
    • Mind-body connection
    • Breathing techniques
  • Ballet:
    • Precise movement control
    • Posture improvement
    • Grace and coordination

Best Practices

  • Safety:
    • Start with basic exercises
    • Practice near a wall or stable support
    • Progress gradually
  • Frequency:
    • Practice balance exercises daily
    • Short sessions (10-15 minutes) are effective
    • Incorporate into daily routine
  • Progression:
    • Start with stable surfaces
    • Advance to unstable surfaces
    • Add movement patterns

Important Considerations

  • Always maintain proper form
  • Stop if you experience pain
  • Consult instructor for correct technique in specialized activities
  • Be patient - balance improves gradually
