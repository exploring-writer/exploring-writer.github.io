Benefits of Workout - Other than Fat Loss and Muscle Building

  • Sweat-inducing exercise increases skin collagen. In addition, strength training thickens dermis layer as well.
  • Heart-rate-elevating exercise increases blood circulation, which improves overall health and function. For example, it increases the amount of oxygen to brain.
  • Light jogging makes your bones denser and spine joints stronger by helping absorption of nutrients with gentle impact between them.
  • Boosts mood, reduces stress
  • You learn to control your movement, which gives you a more chance to avoid or reduce the damage at falling down.
  • Improves sleep
  • There are tons of other benefits. Please workout.
