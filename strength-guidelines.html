Basic Structure

Workout Components

  • Exercise Selection:
    • Include 8-10 different exercises
    • Target all major muscle groups
    • Mix of compound and isolation exercises
  • Repetitions:
    • 8-12 reps per exercise
    • Maintain proper form throughout
    • Adjust weight to achieve target rep range

Training Schedule

Time and Frequency

  • Session Duration:
    • 30-60 minutes per workout
    • Include warm-up and cool-down
  • Weekly Schedule:
    • 2 sessions per week
    • Allow at least 48 hours between sessions

Rest and Recovery

Importance of Rest

  • Muscle Growth:
    • Muscles grow during rest periods, not during exercise
    • Adequate rest is essential for strength gains
  • Between Sets:
    • Take sufficient rest between exercises
    • Allow muscles to recover before next set
  • Between Sessions:
    • Give muscles time to repair and strengthen
    • Avoid training same muscle groups on consecutive days

Best Practices

  • Form and Technique:
    • Focus on proper form over weight
    • Control movements throughout the range of motion
    • Breathe steadily during exercises
  • Progressive Overload:
    • Gradually increase weight or reps
    • Track your progress
    • Listen to your body's signals
  • Nutrition Support:
    • Ensure adequate protein intake
    • Stay hydrated before, during, and after workouts
    • Consider post-workout nutrition timing
