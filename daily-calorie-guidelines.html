Daily Calorie Guidelines

1. Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) Calculation

For Women:

655.1 + (9.56 × weight_kg) + (1.85 × height_cm) - (4.68 × age)

For Men:

66.47 + (13.75 × weight_kg) + (5 × height_cm) - (6.76 × age)

2. Daily Calorie Needs (Maintenance)

Calculate using standard weight × activity factor:

  • 25-30: Sedentary work (desk job)
  • 30-35: Standing work
  • 35-40: Heavy physical labor
  • 40+: Athletes

Example Calculation

For a 162 cm woman with desk job:

  • Standard weight: 55 kg
  • Daily calorie needs: 55 × [25-30] = 1375-1650 kcal
  • Note: If BMR is higher than this range due to current weight, use at least BMR as minimum

3. Weight Loss Calorie Calculation

  • 1 kg fat = 7000 kcal
  • For 0.5 kg/week loss: Create 3000 kcal deficit per week
  • Daily deficit needed: 3000/7 ≈ 429 kcal

Example Weight Loss Plan

For 1600 kcal maintenance:

  • Exercise: 200 kcal/day average
  • Diet: Reduce to 1400 kcal/day
  • Total daily deficit: ~400 kcal

Important Notes

  • Avoid rapid weight loss (>1 kg/week):
    • Harmful to organs
    • Reduces skin elasticity
  • Never eat below BMR:
    • Lowers metabolic rate
    • Counterproductive for weight loss
  • Recommended: 0.5 kg or less weight loss per week
