Waist Circumference Guidelines

Recommended Range

  • Women: Under 85 cm (34 inches)
  • Men: Under 90 cm (36 inches)
  • May vary by overall body size, ethnicity, and other factors. Above measurements are for Koreans.

What does it tell?

  • Waist circumference is an indicator of visceral fat (fat inside belly)
  • Visceral fat is necessary as it lubricates between organs, however, too much amount is associated with increased risk of health problems.
  • Visceral fat is known to secrete various hormones that cause inflammation, insulin resistance (increases risk of diabetes), and leptin resistance (increases appetite).
  • "Anything that can increase visceral fat can accelerate your aging", one of my doctor said

How to Measure

  1. Stand straight with feet shoulder-width apart
  2. Find your waist - the dip above your belly button and below your ribcage
  3. Measure around the waist when exhale
