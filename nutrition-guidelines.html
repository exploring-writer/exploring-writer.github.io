Eating Habits

For Gut & Blood Health

To minimize insulin secretion, reduce load of digestive guts, etc

  • Eat at regular times with intervals
  • Be consistent with portion sizes
  • Avoid overeating

Meal Timing & Digestion

  • Finish your last meal at least 3-4 hours (varies by individual) before bedtime in order to finish digestion before sleep
  • If you must eat late, eat less than usual amount to shorten digestion duration

Low-Carb Diet Considerations

While low-carb diets are popular for health, consider these important points:

  • Brain Health Impact: The brain requires carbohydrates for optimal function. Severe carb restriction can lead to brain damage.
  • Essential Nutrients: All essential nutrients are necessary for health. Understanding and maintaining appropriate amounts is crucial.
  • Detailed Guidelines: For specific portion recommendations, refer to the meal planning cards in the detailed section.
